Quilling

Quilling is an art form that involves the use of strips of paper that are rolled, shaped, and glued together to create decorative designs. During leisure time, i really love to do this activity. even if some people find this quilling hobby challenging and tough. But, when the work I want to do materialises, I am overjoyed. My family and friends like the quilling I give them as gifts.
Tools: Paper, Glue, and Quilling Tools.
