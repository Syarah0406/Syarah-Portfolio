Congkak

Congkak is a traditional Malay game often played as an indoor activity or as a competition. Often, it uses hollow perforated wooden planks or holes on the ground and usually a ‘buah’ (game piece) using rubber seeds or marbles. In a Congkak set, there are two components which are the Congkak board and Congkak piece. The board are shaped like boats and can be made from different types of wood or by digging holes on the ground. There are five to seven inline holes known as 'kampung' (village) and at both ends there is a main hole known as 'rumah'(home). The lenght of the 7 holes Congkak board is usually around 80 cm wide and the diameter of the seven holes is usually about 18 cm.
